Your questions, answered

What is the difference between damage protection and liability insurance?

While AirCover for Hosts protects you while you are hosting an Airbnb stay or Experience, it is not a substitute for personal insurance. You may also be required by law to maintain certain auto insurance, which would not be satisfied by AirCover for Hosts. Since everyone situation is different, you should talk to your insurer to see how or if your policy overlaps with AirCover for Hosts.

How do I become an Airbnb host?

To become an Airbnb host, you'll need to sign up on the Airbnb platform and create a listing for your space or experience. You can set your own availability, pricing, and house rules. Airbnb provides tools and resources to help you get started and connect with potential guests.

How does Airbnb payment and pricing work?

Airbnb handles payment processing for reservations. Guests pay through Airbnb, and hosts receive the payout through the platform. You can set your own pricing and determine additional costs, such as cleaning fees or security deposits, as part of your listing.

What are the cancellation policies on Airbnb?

Airbnb offers various cancellation policies for hosts to choose from, including strict, moderate, and flexible policies. These policies determine the refund amount a guest receives if they cancel a reservation. Hosts can select the policy that suits their preferences and needs.

What are the different types of Airbnb listings?

Airbnb offers various types of listings, including entire homes, private rooms, shared rooms, and unique accommodations such as treehouses or castles. Hosts can create listings based on the type of space they have available.

How do I handle guest reviews on Airbnb?

You can respond to guest reviews on your listing's page. It's a great way to thank guests for their positive feedback and address any concerns or issues they may have raised. Engaging with reviews helps build trust with future guests.

What is the Airbnb Superhost program?

The Airbnb Superhost program recognizes hosts who provide exceptional hospitality. To become a Superhost, you must meet certain criteria, including a high response rate, excellent ratings, and a history of hosting with Airbnb.

Can I set house rules for my Airbnb listing?

Yes, you can set house rules for your Airbnb listing. You can specify rules related to smoking, pets, parties, and more. Guests will see these rules before booking, helping ensure a smooth experience for both parties.

How do I handle a cancellation as an Airbnb host?

As a host, you can choose a cancellation policy for your listing. If a guest cancels, you'll follow the policy you've selected. Be sure to communicate with the guest and Airbnb's support team as needed to resolve the cancellation.
